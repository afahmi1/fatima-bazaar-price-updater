How it works

  • First time an item is seen, you set weight or quantity and its SKU (scan the barcode, or N/A). Then it's remembered.
  • Matching items pull category & details from your Toast inventory.
  • Cost is figured per lb (weight) or per item (quantity), plus the state markup (CA +5%, else +10%).
  • Sell price = adjusted cost ÷ 0.625, then rounded up to the next .49 or .99.
  • Output is one Toast Item Library CSV that updates existing prices and adds new items in a single import.
